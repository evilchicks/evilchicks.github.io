Road Map

Art Creation

Development

Launch

We Launch our Website to the world along with Social media ads (facebook and google) and braodcasting. We also have a unique strategy to allow everyone to be part of the community by joining Whitelisting via multiple ways.

Seed Fund Mint

We are allowing few of the handpicked Members to be a part of the Seed Funders who have the benefit of buying the NFTs at only 0.08 ETH with the minimum of 3 NFTs and Maximum of 5. We are providing only 100 slots for the same. All of the members will recieve total of 30% of the Royality rewards.

PreSale Mint

Metaverse Game Development

Aquiring the Metaverse and Development Game begins. The Artists have been working on the Map and Gameplay which will be revealed on the Social Media in form of the Trailers and 3D models to update the community on our Advances.

Holder Benefits

Revenue Distribution Model is launched where a Part of profits recieved from the Game is distributed amoung the HOLDERS of the EvilChicks. The HOLDERs will also have access to attend all the Virtual/Physical Events, Alpha and Beta releases for feedback.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the Whitelist to Mint?

We have numerous ways so you dont miss the change to get onboard, Along with community participation we have a Limited slots for 'Reserve NFTs' option which will allow you to pay 20% of the Mint price and Reserve yourself NFTs which you can Mint on the Pre-Sale day with the rest 80% of the cost.

What Blockchain is the project hosted on?

The project is hosted on Ethereum Mainet.

What are the ways to own a EvilChicks NFT?

You can Mint in Seed Fund Phase or a Whitelist Mint Phase, Just wait for the Raffle Registration. We will get in touch via our official Email and Discord Announcement channel only.

Will there be a Public Mint?

We are aiming for Pre-Sale only. Please participate in the Mint via Whitelist only. If we decide to allow Public Mint based on the community. Same will be held with the remaining NFTs from the Pre-Sales.

What is Raffle?

Raffle is a way of showing us that you are interested in the Pre-sale/Seed-fund Sale. Once Raffle is completed you will be able to Mint at Pre-sale based on your Selection.
